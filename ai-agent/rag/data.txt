🔹 ACCOUNT & BALANCE

You can check your account balance from the dashboard or by asking the AI assistant.

Example:
"What is my balance?"

The system will securely fetch your latest balance from your account.


🔹 DEPOSIT MONEY

To deposit money:

1. Click on "Deposit" on the dashboard
2. A popup will appear
3. Enter the amount
4. Click "Confirm Deposit"

The amount will be added instantly to your account.


🔹 WITHDRAW MONEY

To withdraw money:

1. Click on "Withdraw"
2. Enter the amount in the popup
3. Click "Confirm Withdraw"

Make sure you have sufficient balance before withdrawing.


🔹 TRANSFER MONEY

To transfer money:

1. Click on "Transfer"
2. Enter:
   - Receiver Account Number
   - Amount
3. Click "Confirm Transfer"

Rules:
- Amount must be greater than 0
- You cannot transfer to your own account
- If amount is very high, confirmation is required
- If balance is insufficient, transfer will be blocked


🔹 TRANSACTION HISTORY

You can view transactions in two ways:

1. Click "Transactions" from the header
2. Click "Transactions" card on dashboard

Features:
- View recent transactions
- Filter by amount
- View today's transactions
- See sent vs received insights


🔹 PROFILE MANAGEMENT

To view or edit profile:

1. Click on your profile icon (top right)
2. Open profile card
3. Click "Edit Profile"

You can update:
- Name
- Profile picture


🔹 SUPPORT / TICKET SYSTEM

If you face any issue:

1. Type: "create ticket"
2. Describe your issue
3. Confirm the ticket

Example:
"I am unable to see transactions"

System will:
- Create a support ticket
- Assign an agent
- Track status


🔹 TRANSACTION FAILURE

If a transaction fails:
- Money is automatically refunded
- Refund may take up to 24 hours


🔹 LIMITS & RULES

- Minimum balance for savings account is ₹2000
- ATM withdrawal limit per day is ₹50000
- Charges after ATM limit: ₹20 per transaction
- NEFT works from 8 AM to 7 PM (working days)
- IMPS works 24x7


🔹 AI ASSISTANT CAPABILITIES

The AI assistant can:

- Check balance
- Show transactions
- Transfer money
- Detect unusual transactions
- Create support tickets
- Answer banking questions

It understands:
- English
- Hindi
- Mixed language queries


🔹 SECURITY & SAFETY

- All transactions require validation
- High-value transactions need confirmation
- Fraud detection is applied before transfer
- User must confirm critical actions
